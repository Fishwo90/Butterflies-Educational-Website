The Amazing Transformation of Butterflies

What is Metamorphosis?

Metamorphosis is a process in which insects completely transform their appearance in 4 stages: Egg, Larva, Pupa, and Adult.

Why are Butterflies important?

Butterflies are pollinators, providing food sources for birds, bats, and other insects.

The Four Stages of Development

Egg Stage: Cream-colored eggs laid under Milkweed leaves.

Larva Stage: Caterpillar feeds heavily on Milkweed.

Pupa Stage: Transformation inside a Chrysalis.

Adult Stage: Fully developed butterfly emerges.
